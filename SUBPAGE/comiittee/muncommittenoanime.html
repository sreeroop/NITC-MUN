UNHRC:

The United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC) is a major institution focusing on protection and promotion of human rights. It addresses global humanitarian crises and makes recommendations regarding human rights violations. This committee will emulate some of the problems that the UNHRC has had to mitigate, i.e. issues faced in the form of discrimination and oppression As a delegate of UNHRC, your focus will be working together analysing human rights issues across the globe and drafting possible solutions for the same.

ECOSOC:

The Economic and Social Council is one of the six main organs of the United Nations that focuses on the three pillars of sustainable development - Society, Economics, and Environment. The council is a central forum for discussing social and economic policies and plays a key role in enriching international cooperation . ECOSOC acts as a junction between the UN and the civil society by promoting dialogue among policymakers, parliamentarians, businesses, and over 3000 NGOs,thereby widening the UN network.
