DO's

• Delegates should wear formal dress during the entire conference.
• Be respectful in both spoken and written language at all times.
• Be courteous towards the Executive board and fellow delegates.
• The video should be switched on during the conference.
• Be aware that all sessions will be recorded and all chats can be reviewed at any time by the Executive board and Organising committee.
• Remain in character by consistently advocating the interests and representing the policies of the country assigned.
• Use the virtual background provided by NITCMUN.
• The user ID should be the name of the country you are representing and the profile should be that of the country flag.
• The Delegates *must attend all the sessions in order to be eligible for awards.*
• Delegates should have a charged laptop and headphones, preferably, to cancel out noise.
• Turn off your mics when you're not speaking/ not asked to speak. Video must remain on at all times.
• The decision of EB would be final and binding.





DONT's

• Please refrain from wearing any distracting clothes, jewelry.
• Refrain using any audio. recordings or recorded speech during the debate.
• Engaging in any sort of vulgar, hurtful or extremely condescending comments or language would result in disobedience. Decorum must be maintained at all times.
• A warning would be given, but if the delegate doesn't co-operate, it might lead to suspension of the delegate.
