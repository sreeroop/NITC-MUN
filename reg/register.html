Guidelines for Training Programme by YouthMUN, LSE

1. The sessions are conducted by YouthMUN team on 7th and 14th providing you an opportunity to brush up your MUN skills with guidance.
2. Program covers the basics of MUN, mode of preparation and areas of research. It aims to train the participants in order to perform well in the activity while gaining the most out of it.
3. All MUN participants will receive a certificate of completion of the session attested by NITC and LSE, provided they attend one sesion.
4. Non-MUN participants are also allowed to participate free of cost. They can avail the certificate upon installment of a standard fee.
5. Preference would be given to MUN participants and the those paying for the certificate.
6. The Link for the training programme will be provided 2-3 days prior to the event, via the registered mail id.
