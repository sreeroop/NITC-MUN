About IPF

The Industrial and Planning Forum(IPF) is a techno-management club that strives to groom students for facing the professional world. The aim at IPF is to bridge the gap between technical education and the corporate scene, while simultaneously focusing on the overall personality development.

The Industrial and Planning Forum of NITC is proud to announce its fourth edition of NITCMUN in association with Asian MUN Circuit.
Join us on 27, 28 and 29th of November for our first ever virtual MUN. Time to suit up and prepare, let your wits and words shape the world.
