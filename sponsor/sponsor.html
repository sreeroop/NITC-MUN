Unique World Education

Unique World Education is a GCC based premier coaching institute that helps aspirants in the preparation of competitive exams such as NEET, JEE and IAS. With the mission to help students and young professionals prepare for success, they have established a strong presence in the UAE. U.W.E offers a 2 year comprehensive programme that caters to the need of engineering and medical aspirants. Their rigorous coaching will help the students secure a seat in a prestigious institution. A building is as strong as its foundation, and so is your future. Keeping this fact in mind, U.W.E also offers foundational courses for students from grade 8-10, that'll strengthen their basics, be it science or mathematics. In addition to the variety of programs and courses offered by Unique World Education, special tuition batches are designed to students from grade 8 onwards. The tuition classes include worksheets, notes and practice tests; all under the guidance of qualified teachers that helps the students get a complete learning experience.
